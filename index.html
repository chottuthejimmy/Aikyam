AIKYAM-2025

AIKYAM 2025 is the annual fest of the Department of AI & ML, JSS Academy of Technical Education, Bangalore. It is a vibrant celebration of technology, innovation, and culture, bringing together students, professionals, and enthusiasts. The fest features technical workshops, hackathons, e-sports tournaments, cultural performances, and engaging competitions. With a focus on collaboration and creativity, AIKYAM provides a platform for students to showcase their talents and connect with industry leaders.
